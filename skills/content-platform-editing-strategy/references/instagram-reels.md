# Instagram Reels

Public Instagram and Meta materials describe Reels ranking and recommendations as prediction systems influenced by viewer activity, content information, engagement, eligibility, and personalization. Treat tactical advice as a dated hypothesis and run a current-source check for latest Reels ranking, creator, or policy rules. Do not claim secret ranking weights.

## Editing bias

Instagram Reels usually needs strong visual taste, shareability, save value, and creator or brand trust. It can use fast hooks, but the final cut should still feel visually coherent and grid-safe.

Good structures:

- Visual result -> creator context -> useful steps -> save/share CTA.
- Aesthetic scene -> problem -> proof -> concise recommendation.
- Brand/product reveal -> use case -> social proof -> soft action.

## Content strategy

- `audienceIntent`: be inspired, save an idea, send to a friend, evaluate a product, or trust a creator/brand.
- `contentAngle`: aesthetic result, creator context, mini tutorial, lifestyle proof, brand story, or recommendation.
- `scriptPromise`: a visually coherent reason to watch, save, or share.
- `proofAsset`: polished first frame, cover-safe crop, result shot, process, creator credibility, or social proof.
- `interactionDesign`: save, share/send, profile visit, follow, or soft purchase cue.
- `editingImplication`: keep visual taste and readability; do not add clutter just to chase retention.

## Evidence to inspect

- First frame and cover crop for profile grid and feed context.
- Whether the video has a shareable or save-worthy reason.
- Visual consistency: color, typography, composition, and caption density.
- Creator credibility or brand safety.
- Reels eligibility risks: low-quality repost feel, watermarks, misleading claims.

## Avoid

- Cluttered overlays that reduce visual polish.
- Copying TikTok-native chaos into a premium or lifestyle brand cut.
- Missing cover/title consideration.
- Unsupported brand, endorsement, health, beauty, or financial claims.
- Treating likes as the only success signal when saves and shares matter.
