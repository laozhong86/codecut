# Douyin

Use this as a China-market dated hypothesis. Run a current-source check for latest Douyin creator, commerce, search, policy, or traffic guidance. Do not claim secret ranking weights.

## Editing bias

Douyin usually rewards fast comprehension, visible payoff, native entertainment, strong proof, and tight commerce relevance. For product or service content, lead with the result, pain point, or proof shot before explanation.

Good structures:

- Result or conflict -> proof -> demonstration -> one action.
- Pain point -> quick reveal -> process -> outcome.
- Search-intent answer -> proof -> condensed steps -> save or buy cue.

## Content strategy

- `audienceIntent`: fast answer, entertainment, product proof, local need, or purchase confidence.
- `contentAngle`: result-first, mistake correction, comparison, practical demo, or creator reaction.
- `scriptPromise`: one clear reason to keep watching, preferably tied to visible result or proof.
- `proofAsset`: demo, comparison, product close-up, receipt, packaging, before/after, or real-use scene.
- `interactionDesign`: comment for real debate, search follow-up for demand capture, buy/consult only after proof.
- `editingImplication`: show payoff early, compress setup, keep proof legible, and avoid overstacked CTA.

## Evidence to inspect

- First-frame product, face, result, or scene clarity.
- Transcript strength in the first 1-3 seconds.
- Proof shots: demo, comparison, receipt, packaging, before/after, or real usage.
- Search keywords in title, speech, caption, and overlays.
- Whether claims have visual or spoken evidence.

## Avoid

- Applying generic TikTok humor when the business goal is Douyin commerce trust.
- Slow brand intros before proof.
- Unsupported price, guarantee, delivery, or official-platform claims.
- Dense lower-right overlays that conflict with platform UI.
- Treating third-party traffic-pool numbers as current official truth.
