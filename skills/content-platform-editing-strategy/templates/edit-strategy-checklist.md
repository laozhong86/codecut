# Edit Strategy Checklist

Use this checklist before turning a `PlatformStrategyBrief` into a CodeCut `EditingDecisionLedger`.

- Platform and business outcome are explicit.
- Latest or numeric platform claims have a current-source check.
- Platform claims are labeled as dated hypothesis when not verified.
- Transcript evidence has been inspected or marked missing.
- Visual proof has been inspected or marked missing.
- Product, price, guarantee, health, beauty, finance, or policy claims have evidence.
- First-frame recommendation names the source evidence.
- Hook candidates are tied to transcript or visual proof.
- Structure recommendation names one primary signal, not every possible metric.
- CTA matches the platform and business outcome.
- Safe-zone and cover/title constraints are named when relevant.
- `materialAudit`, `storyBeats`, `candidateClips`, `selectedStructure`, and `qaChecklist` are ready for CodeCut planning.
- The strategy does not mutate the timeline or call executor tools.
